Biryani Recipe

Chicken Biryani that can be cooked at home by anyone

Ingredients

  • Basmati Rice
  • Chicken
  • Curd
  • Spices
  • Chilli
  • Mint

Steps

  1. Marinate the chicken in the spices and curd
  2. Partially cook the rice(75%)
  3. Cook the marinated chicken
  4. When the chicken is almost cooked, Add the partially cooked rice over it
  5. Cover and cook on the lowest heat for 10 minutes
  6. Garnish and serve
